Top Cybersecurity Tips

Use Strong Passwords

Create complex passwords and change them regularly to keep your accounts secure.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your accounts with two-factor authentication (2FA).

Avoid Phishing Emails

Be cautious of suspicious emails and never click on unknown links or attachments.

Keep Software Updated

Always update your operating system, apps, and antivirus to protect against vulnerabilities.

Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Fraudulent emails or messages that trick you into revealing personal information.

Malware

Malicious software that can damage your system or steal sensitive data.

Ransomware

Software that locks your files and demands payment to restore access.

Social Engineering

Manipulation techniques to trick people into giving confidential information.
